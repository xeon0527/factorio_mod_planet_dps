Version: 0.2.0
Date: 14. 7. 2026
  Major Features:
    - Renamed the Promethium Subcore to the DPS Subcore.
    - The DPS Subcore now generates DPS Credits.
    - DPS Scrap now generates as surface ore deposits. DPS Credits now serve as the primary resource currency for progression on the DPS planet. However, you will need a great deal of them.
    - The recycling results for DPS Scrap have been adjusted to be more favorable.
    - Added the DPS Credit Translator. This building can instantly convert DPS Credit into useful resources.
    - Made minor adjustments to the overall technology tree.
    - Removed the DPS Science Pack and added the DPS Data Pack.
    - D.O.R.A.X no longer serves any function. Content for D.O.R.A.X will be added in a future update.

  Minor Features:
    - The range of the Supersonic Grenade Launcher has been increased to 36.
    - Removed compatibility with K2 Spaced Out.
